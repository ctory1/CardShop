About the Shop

Built by Joshua and Colin for collectors who like the hunt.

J&C PokéPawns is a local card shop idea with two lanes: Pokémon singles for collectors and casual chess for anyone who wants a good game.

Joshua W.

Joshua keeps the chess table active and helps with card organization, pricing, and grading prep so customers know what they are looking at.

Colin T.

Colin focuses on the Pokémon side: tracking market value, spotting clean binder hits, and helping buyers find cards that fit a collection or deck goal.

Our Area

Find us in La Crosse.

We serve collectors and chess players around La Crosse, Wisconsin. Use the map to get oriented, then contact Joshua or Colin for the latest meet-up details.
