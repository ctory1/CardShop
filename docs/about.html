About the Shop

Built by a lifelong Pokémon collector for collectors.

CardShop Collectables is in the process of becoming a trusted online marketplace for Pokémon collectors and chess enthusiasts.

Colin Toryfter

Hi! I'm Colin, a 20-year-old lifelong Pokémon collector and the founder of CardShop Collectables. I've spent many years buying, selling, trading, grading, and researching Pokémon cards, and one of my favorite parts of the hobby is finding hidden gems and helping other collectors grow their collections.

Alongside collecting, I'm also passionate about chess and enjoy helping players of all skill levels improve their game. Whether you're searching for your next favorite card or looking for someone to play a game of chess with, I hope CardShop Collectables becomes a welcoming place for the community.

My long-term vision is to grow CardShop Collectables into more than just a website. I want to build a trusted online marketplace where collectors can create profiles, showcase their collections, buy and sell cards, negotiate trades and deals directly with one another, and build lasting connections within the Pokémon community.

Every feature added to this website is another step toward that goal. Thanks for being part of the journey!
