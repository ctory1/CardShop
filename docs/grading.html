Grading Service

Know what is worth submitting.

We help collectors inspect cards, protect them properly, and decide which ones make sense for grading.

01

$5 Grade Estimate

Pay $5 upfront and we inspect the card in front of you using PSA's standards, explaining centering, corners, edges, surface, print lines, dents, and likely grade range.

02

Upfront Opinion

We give a realistic opinion before you submit, such as "PSA 9 is most probable," so you can decide if grading makes sense.

03

$20 PSA Send-Off

If you want to move forward, add $20 and we package the card in front of you, protect it properly, and send it to PSA.

Collector Friendly

Bring one card or a full stack.

We can help sort your binder into keep, sell, trade, and grade piles. Our upfront estimate is based on PSA's standards, but it is our best opinion, not a guaranteed PSA result, and we will explain the risk before recommending a submission.
