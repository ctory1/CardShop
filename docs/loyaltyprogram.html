What counts?

  • Pokemon singles, sealed products, chess items, and grading-service fees count toward rewards.
  • Reward credit is earned in $100 blocks and rounded down to the nearest full block.
  • Credits can be stacked, but they are used on future purchases only.
  • Store credit, refunds, and taxes do not earn additional rewards.
